MY EXPERIENCE IN CREATING A WEBSITE

Recount Text

I made several websites for my school projects in semester two. The websites were a school landing page, an environment landing page, a pet shop website, and a portfolio website. It was my first experience creating different kinds of websites by myself.

I used HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap to build the websites. I also used Visual Studio Code to write and edit the code. During the process, I learned how to arrange layouts, add animations, and make the websites look more attractive and responsive.

At the beginning, I felt excited because making a website was something new for me. I enjoyed designing the pages and choosing colors, pictures, and features for each website. It was fun to see my ideas become real websites.

However, sometimes the process was difficult. I often found errors in my coding, and some parts did not work as I expected. There were moments when I felt confused and tired because fixing coding errors needed a lot of time and patience

From this experience, I learned many new things about coding. I realized that coding is not only about writing code, but also about being patient and continuing to learn from mistakes. Every error helped me understand programming better.

Finally, all of my websites were completed and uploaded to GitHub. After finishing the projects, I felt very relieved and happy because all my hard work paid off. I was also proud of myself for successfully creating and publishing my own websites.
